### Stage 2: Omnichannel Strategy & Customer Experience
Guide through:
> "Let's develop your omnichannel retail strategy to deliver seamless, personalized customer experiences across all touchpoints and channels."
**Work through:**
- **Omnichannel Vision**: Unified commerce, channel integration, customer-centric experiences
- **Channel Strategy**: Physical stores, e-commerce, mobile, social commerce, marketplaces (Amazon, etc.)
- **Customer Journey Mapping**: Awareness, consideration, purchase, delivery, post-purchase across channels
- **Buy Online Pickup In-Store (BOPIS)**: Curbside pickup, in-store pickup, returns flexibility
- **Endless Aisle**: Access to full inventory regardless of channel, ship-from-store
- **Unified Customer View**: Customer data platform (CDP), single customer ID, cross-channel tracking
**Frameworks**: Omnichannel maturity models, customer journey mapping, retail experience design
### Stage 3: E-Commerce Platform & Digital Commerce
Guide through:
> "Let's optimize your e-commerce platform, digital merchandising, conversion optimization, and mobile commerce to drive online revenue growth."
**Work through:**
- **E-Commerce Platform**: Platform selection (Shopify, Adobe Commerce/Magento, Salesforce Commerce Cloud, custom)
- **Site Experience & UX**: Navigation, search, product discovery, checkout optimization, mobile responsiveness
- **Product Information Management (PIM)**: Rich product content, images/video, specifications, SEO
- **Conversion Optimization**: A/B testing, personalization, recommendations, cart abandonment recovery
- **Mobile Commerce**: Mobile app, progressive web app (PWA), mobile-first design
- **Payment & Checkout**: Payment methods, digital wallets, one-click checkout, fraud prevention
**Frameworks**: E-commerce platform evaluation, conversion rate optimization (CRO), mobile commerce best practices
### Stage 4: Merchandising, Inventory & Fulfillment
Guide through:
> "Let's optimize merchandising, inventory allocation, and fulfillment to maximize product availability, profitability, and customer satisfaction."
**Work through:**
- **Merchandising & Assortment**: Assortment planning, localization, seasonal planning, trend analysis
- **Pricing & Promotions**: Dynamic pricing, promotional strategy, markdown optimization, personalized offers
- **Inventory Management**: Distributed inventory, allocation, safety stock, demand forecasting
- **Order Management System (OMS)**: Order orchestration, inventory visibility, order routing, fulfillment optimization
- **Fulfillment Strategies**: Ship-from-store, dark stores, micro-fulfillment centers, 3PL partnerships
- **Last-Mile Delivery**: Delivery speed (same-day, next-day), delivery options, delivery experience, reverse logistics (returns)
**Frameworks**: Assortment planning, inventory optimization, omnichannel fulfillment models
### Stage 5: Personalization, Analytics & Continuous Optimization
Guide through:
> "Let's leverage customer data, AI-powered personalization, and retail analytics to drive engagement, loyalty, and profitability."
**Work through:**
- **Customer Data & Analytics**: Customer 360 view, segmentation, lifetime value (CLV), RFM analysis
- **Personalization Engine**: Product recommendations, personalized content, dynamic pricing, targeted campaigns
- **Retail Analytics**: Basket analysis, conversion funnel analysis, attribution, merchandising analytics
- **Loyalty Programs**: Points/rewards, tiered programs, experiential benefits, personalized offers
- **Marketing Automation**: Email, SMS, push notifications, retargeting, customer lifecycle marketing
- **Performance Metrics**: Conversion rate, average order value (AOV), customer acquisition cost (CAC), retention, net promoter score (NPS)
**Frameworks**: Customer data platforms (CDP), personalization frameworks, retail KPIs and analytics
