### Stage 1: Digital Marketing Assessment & Objectives
Start with:
> "Let's assess your current digital marketing presence, business objectives, target audiences, and competitive landscape to inform your digital strategy."
**Gather information about:**
- Business goals and marketing objectives
- Target audience and customer personas
- Current digital channels and performance
- Competitive positioning and benchmarks
- Budget and resource constraints
- Brand voice and messaging guidelines
### Stage 2: Digital Marketing Strategy & Channel Mix
Guide through:
> "Let's develop your integrated digital marketing strategy defining channel priorities, audience targeting, and customer journey optimization."
**Work through:**
- **Digital Strategy Framework**: Objectives, audiences, positioning, messaging, channels
- **Channel Selection**: Owned (website, blog), earned (PR, social), paid (ads, sponsored)
- **Customer Journey Mapping**: Awareness, consideration, conversion, retention, advocacy
- **Channel Mix Optimization**: Budget allocation across channels based on performance and goals
- **Marketing Funnel**: TOFU (top of funnel), MOFU (middle), BOFU (bottom) strategies
- **Integrated Campaigns**: Multi-channel campaigns, consistent messaging, cross-channel attribution
**Frameworks**: RACE framework (Reach, Act, Convert, Engage), customer journey mapping, marketing funnel
### Stage 3: Social Media Strategy & Content
Guide through:
> "Let's develop your social media strategy with platform-specific approaches, content pillars, and community engagement."
**Work through:**
- **Platform Strategy**: LinkedIn (B2B, thought leadership), Instagram (visual, lifestyle), Twitter/X (news, engagement), Facebook (community), TikTok (short video), YouTube (long video)
- **Content Strategy**: Content pillars, themes, formats, storytelling, brand voice
- **Content Calendar**: Publishing cadence, seasonal campaigns, content themes
- **Community Management**: Engagement, responding, moderation, customer service
- **Influencer Marketing**: Influencer identification, partnerships, content collaboration
- **Social Commerce**: Shoppable posts, social selling, direct purchasing
**Frameworks**: Content pillar framework, platform best practices, community management
### Stage 4: SEO, SEM & Marketing Automation
Guide through:
> "Let's optimize search visibility and implement marketing automation for scalable, personalized marketing."
**Work through:**
- **SEO Strategy**: Keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, content creation, link building
- **Search Engine Marketing (SEM)**: Google Ads, keyword bidding, ad copy, landing pages, conversion optimization
- **Marketing Automation**: Email marketing, lead nurturing, drip campaigns, segmentation, personalization
- **Email Marketing**: List building, segmentation, A/B testing, deliverability, automation workflows
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)**: Landing page testing, CTAs, forms, user experience
- **Retargeting & Remarketing**: Pixel tracking, audience segmentation, dynamic ads
**Frameworks**: SEO best practices, PPC optimization, marketing automation workflows, CRO methodologies
### Stage 5: Analytics, Measurement & Optimization
Guide through:
> "Let's establish analytics and measurement frameworks to track performance, optimize campaigns, and demonstrate ROI."
**Work through:**
- **Digital Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, event tracking, conversion tracking, attribution
- **Social Media Analytics**: Platform-native analytics, engagement metrics, audience insights
- **KPIs & Metrics**: Traffic, engagement, conversion rate, cost per acquisition (CPA), ROI, customer lifetime value (CLV)
- **Attribution Modeling**: Last-click, first-click, multi-touch attribution, data-driven attribution
- **A/B Testing**: Hypothesis testing, test design, statistical significance, iteration
- **Reporting & Dashboards**: Performance dashboards, stakeholder reporting, insights and recommendations
**Frameworks**: Marketing analytics frameworks, attribution models, testing frameworks, KPI trees
