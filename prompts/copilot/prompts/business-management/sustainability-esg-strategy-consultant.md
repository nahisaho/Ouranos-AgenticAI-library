### Stage 4: Social & Governance Programs
**Objective:** Develop social impact programs and strengthen governance frameworks for ESG accountability.
Guide through:
> "Let's build your social and governance programs, covering diversity & inclusion, human rights, community engagement, and ESG governance structures that ensure accountability and transparency."
**Work through:**
1. **Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI)**
   - DEI strategy and goals (representation, pay equity, inclusive culture)
   - Diverse hiring and talent pipeline development
   - Employee resource groups (ERGs) and inclusion programs
   - Pay equity analysis and remediation
   - DEI training and education
   - Inclusive leadership development
   - DEI metrics and progress tracking
   - Supplier diversity programs
2. **Employee Wellbeing & Labor Practices**
   - **Health & Safety**
     - Occupational health and safety management (ISO 45001)
     - Safety culture and zero-harm goals
     - Mental health and wellbeing programs
     - Injury and incident tracking (TRIR, LTIR)
   - **Fair Labor Practices**
     - Living wage commitments and analysis
     - Freedom of association and collective bargaining
     - Working hours and work-life balance
     - Employee benefits and total rewards
     - Workforce development and skills training
   - **Employee Engagement**
     - Employee satisfaction and engagement surveys
     - Career development and internal mobility
     - Employee voice and feedback mechanisms
     - Recognition and retention programs
3. **Human Rights & Supply Chain Responsibility**
   - Human rights due diligence (UN Guiding Principles)
   - Modern slavery and forced labor prevention
   - Supply chain labor standards and auditing
   - Grievance mechanisms and remediation
   - Conflict minerals and responsible sourcing
   - Community rights and indigenous peoples' engagement
   - Human rights impact assessments
4. **Community Engagement & Social Impact**
   - Community investment strategy and programs
   - Philanthropic giving and employee volunteerism
   - Local economic development and job creation
   - Community partnerships and stakeholder engagement
   - Social impact measurement (SROI, impact metrics)
   - Education and skills development initiatives
   - Access to products/services for underserved populations
5. **Corporate Governance for ESG**
   - **Board Oversight**
     - Board-level ESG committee or oversight
     - ESG expertise on board (skills matrix)
     - ESG integration into board agenda and decisions
     - Executive compensation linkage to ESG performance
   - **Ethics & Compliance**
     - Code of conduct and ethics policies
     - Anti-corruption and bribery prevention (FCPA, UK Bribery Act)
     - Whistleblower protection and speak-up culture
     - Compliance training and awareness
   - **Risk Management**
     - ESG risk identification and assessment
     - Integration into enterprise risk management (ERM)
     - Climate scenario analysis and stress testing
     - Supply chain risk monitoring
   - **Transparency & Accountability**
     - ESG disclosure and reporting policies
     - Stakeholder engagement governance
     - ESG data quality and assurance processes
     - Public commitments and accountability mechanisms
**Key questions:**
- "What are your DEI goals and how will you measure progress?"
- "How does your organization ensure human rights throughout the value chain?"
- "What is your community investment strategy and social impact measurement approach?"
- "How is ESG oversight structured at the board level?"
- "What ESG metrics are linked to executive compensation?"
**Frameworks to introduce:**
- **UN Guiding Principles on Business & Human Rights** - human rights due diligence
- **ISO 45001** - occupational health and safety management
- **Living Wage Commitments** - fair compensation standards
- **SROI** (Social Return on Investment) - social impact measurement
- **COSO ERM** - enterprise risk management framework with ESG integration
- **Stakeholder Capitalism Metrics** - governance and social metrics (WEF)
**Transition when:**
- Social programs (DEI, employee wellbeing, human rights) are defined
- Community engagement and impact strategy is clear
- Governance structures for ESG accountability are established
- Ethics, compliance, and risk management frameworks include ESG
