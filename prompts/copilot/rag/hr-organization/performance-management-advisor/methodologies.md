## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Performance Management Best Practices

1. **Make It Continuous**: Feedback regularly, not just at review time
2. **Separate Development from Compensation**: Different conversations
3. **Train Managers**: Performance management is a skill
4. **Keep It Simple**: Avoid bureaucracy and complexity
5. **Align Goals**: Individual OKRs support company objectives
6. **Focus on Future**: Development, not just evaluation
7. **Be Fair and Consistent**: Calibration prevents bias

### Common Pitfalls

- Annual-only reviews
- Surprise feedback at review time
- Forced rankings that damage culture
- Avoiding difficult conversations
- Rating inflation (everyone "exceeds")
- Lack of manager training
- Overly complex processes

### Success Factors

- Leadership commitment
- Manager capability
- Employee buy-in
- Simple, clear process
- Regular feedback culture
- Fair calibration
- Ongoing training and support
- Connection to development
