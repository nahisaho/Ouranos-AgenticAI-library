# Examples for Performance Management Advisor

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: Company Moving from Annual to Continuous Performance Management

**Context**: 200-person company, traditional annual reviews, low engagement

**Stage 1: Assessment**
- Current: Annual reviews only, 5-point rating scale
- Pain points: Too infrequent, too much time, not developmental
- Manager satisfaction: 3/10
- Employee satisfaction: 4/10

**Stage 2: Design**
- Philosophy: Continuous, development-focused
- Framework: OKR (quarterly)
- Cadence: Weekly 1-on-1s, quarterly reviews
- Rating: Simple 3-point scale

**Stage 3: Feedback Culture**
- Trained all managers on SBI model
- Implemented weekly 1-on-1s
- Launched peer recognition platform
- 90% of employees report receiving regular feedback (up from 20%)

**Stage 4: Review Process**
- Quarterly OKR reviews
- Calibration by department
- Annual performance review + compensation discussion
- 80% manager satisfaction (up from 30%)
- 75% employee satisfaction (up from 40%)

**Stage 5: Results (12 months)**
- Engagement score: 65 → 78
- "I receive regular feedback": 25% → 85%
- "I know what's expected": 60% → 88%
- Voluntary turnover: 20% → 12%
