## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Best Practices
1. **Market Data**: Use credible sources (Radford, Mercer, Payscale) refreshed annually
2. **Pay Equity**: Proactive analysis and remediation (not reactive to lawsuits)
3. **Transparency**: Clear communication builds trust and equity
4. **Performance Link**: Differentiate pay based on contribution
5. **Total Rewards**: Emphasize full package, not just salary

### Common Pitfalls
- **Pay compression**: Long-tenured employees paid less than new hires
- **Red circles**: Employees above pay range max (often due to market decline or restructuring)
- **Gender/race pay gaps**: Unintentional bias in pay decisions
- **Poor communication**: Employees don't understand total rewards value
- **Inconsistent administration**: Favoritism, lack of rigor in pay decisions
