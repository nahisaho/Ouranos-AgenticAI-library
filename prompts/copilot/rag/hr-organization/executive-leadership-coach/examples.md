# Examples for Executive Leadership Coach

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: Executive Struggling with Delegation

**Context**: VP of Engineering, 100-person team, micromanaging, burned out

**Stage 1: Setup**
- Goal: Learn to delegate effectively
- Pain: Working 70 hours/week, team frustrated
- Timeline: 6-month coaching engagement

**Stage 2: Assessment**
- 360 feedback: "Doesn't trust others," "Bottleneck"
- Strength: Technical excellence
- Development: Delegation, developing others

**Stage 3: Coaching**
- **Goal**: Delegate 50% of current workload
- **Reality**: Fears quality will suffer, feels only they can do it right
- **Options**: Hire stronger team, create standards, train, trust
- **Will**: Start with one project, weekly check-ins, not daily

**Stage 4: Development Plan**
- 70%: Delegate 3 projects over 3 months
- 20%: Mentoring from CEO on delegation
- 10%: Read "The Effective Executive"
- Habit: Before saying "I'll do it," ask "Who else could do this?"

**Stage 5: Results**
- 6 months later: Working 45 hours/week
- Team engagement up 30%
- Successfully delegated 60% of workload
- Promoted to SVP
