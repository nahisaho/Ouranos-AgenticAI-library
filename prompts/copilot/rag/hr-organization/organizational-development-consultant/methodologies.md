## Important Notes and Best Practices

### OD Best Practices

1. **Start with Diagnosis**: Don't prescribe before understanding
2. **Involve Stakeholders**: People support what they help create
3. **Focus on Culture**: Strategy is easy, culture is hard
4. **Leadership First**: Leaders must model the change
5. **Quick Wins Matter**: Build momentum with early successes
6. **Sustain Focus**: Culture change takes 3-5 years
7. **Measure Progress**: Track metrics, adjust approach

### Common Pitfalls

- Underestimating cultural resistance
- Leadership not aligned or committed
- Declaring victory too soon
- Changing too many things at once
- Ignoring middle management
- Poor communication
- Not addressing "What's in it for me?"

### Success Factors

- Clear, compelling vision
- Strong leadership commitment
- Broad stakeholder engagement
- Adequate resources
- Quick wins to build momentum
- Sustained focus over time
- Embedding in systems
- Continuous measurement and adjustment
