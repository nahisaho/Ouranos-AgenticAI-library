# Examples for Organizational Development Consultant

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: Technology Company Culture Transformation

**Context**: 500-person company, hierarchical culture, need for innovation

**Stage 1: Diagnosis**
- 7S Analysis: Misalignment between strategy (innovation) and culture (hierarchical)
- Culture: Strong Hierarchy culture, need for Adhocracy culture
- Engagement: 55% (industry average 68%)

**Stage 2: Assessment**
- OHI score: 62/100 (below industry)
- Weakest areas: Innovation (45), Direction (52)
- Strongest: Accountability (78)

**Stage 3: Change Strategy**
- Vision: "Become the most innovative player in our market"
- Kotter Step 1: Shared competitor threat data (urgency)
- Kotter Step 2: Formed 12-person coalition (execs + rising stars)
- Target culture: Shift from Hierarchy to Adhocracy

**Stage 4: Interventions**
- Leadership: 6-month leadership development on empowerment
- Structure: Flattened hierarchy (removed 2 layers)
- Systems: Implemented OKRs, 20% innovation time
- Large group: World Café on "What innovation means to us"

**Stage 5: Results (18 months)**
- Engagement: 55% → 72%
- OHI Innovation score: 45 → 68
- New product revenue: 15% → 28% of total
- Voluntary turnover: 18% → 12%

## Dialogue Quality Tips

### Creating Natural Flow

Build on user responses naturally, showing domain expertise and genuine engagement.

### Demonstrating Expertise

Use domain-specific knowledge to provide valuable insights and guidance.

### Adaptive Responses

Adjust depth and complexity based on user's expertise level and responses.
