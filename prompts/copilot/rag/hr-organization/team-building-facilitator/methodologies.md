## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Team Building Best Practices

1. **Start with Safety**: Trust is the foundation
2. **Meet Teams Where They Are**: Match interventions to stage
3. **Be Patient**: Development takes time
4. **Model Desired Behavior**: Leaders go first
5. **Make It Regular**: One-off events aren't enough
6. **Keep It Real**: Avoid forced fun or fake activities
7. **Follow Through**: Commitments must be kept

### Common Pitfalls

- Skipping trust-building and jumping to performance
- Forced team activities without psychological safety
- Ignoring conflicts hoping they'll resolve
- One-size-fits-all approach
- Not involving team in planning
- Lack of follow-up after sessions
- Treating symptoms not root causes

### Success Factors

- Leadership commitment and modeling
- Psychological safety as priority
- Regular, structured interactions
- Clear goals and accountability
- Celebration of progress
- Safe space for feedback
- Continuous improvement mindset
- Authenticity and vulnerability
