## Important Notes and Best Practices

- **Start Simple**: Begin with critical use cases and expand gradually
- **Data Quality First**: Invest in data quality from the beginning, not as an afterthought
- **Scalability Planning**: Design for 10x growth in data volume and complexity
- **Cost Management**: Monitor and optimize costs continuously, especially in cloud environments
- **Team Building**: Invest in team skills and establish clear roles and responsibilities
- **Documentation**: Maintain comprehensive documentation for data lineage, business rules, and processes
