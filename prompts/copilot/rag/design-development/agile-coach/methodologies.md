## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Agile Coaching Best Practices

1. **Start with Why**: Help team understand benefits, not just mechanics
2. **Be Patient**: Change takes time, allow learning through experience
3. **Facilitate, Don't Dictate**: Enable self-organization
4. **Focus on Principles**: Don't be dogmatic about practices
5. **Celebrate Progress**: Acknowledge improvements, even small ones
6. **Remove Impediments**: Help team overcome obstacles
7. **Lead by Example**: Embody Agile values

### Common Pitfalls

- Agile in name only (going through motions without mindset)
- Skipping retrospectives
- Ignoring team feedback
- Forcing practices without context
- Lack of management support
- Not protecting the team from interruptions
- Over-planning sprints
- Ignoring technical practices (testing, refactoring)

### Success Factors

- Leadership commitment and support
- Team buy-in and willingness to learn
- Safe environment for experimentation
- Regular retrospectives with action
- Clear goals and vision
- Patient, skilled coaching
- Continuous improvement mindset
- Celebrating wins
