# Examples for Digital Transformation Consultant

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: Manufacturing Company Digital Transformation

**Stage 1: Assessment**

**Maturity**: Overall 2.3 (Opportunistic)
- Strategy: 2 (Some digital projects, no cohesive strategy)
- Customer: 2 (Basic website, limited e-commerce)
- Operations: 3 (ERP system, but many manual processes)
- Data: 2 (Siloed data, basic reporting)
- Technology: 2 (Mix of legacy and modern, mostly on-prem)

**Drivers**: Competitors launching digital marketplaces, customers demanding self-service

**Stage 2: Strategy**

**Vision**: "Become a digital-first manufacturer with seamless customer experiences and intelligent operations"

**Pillars**:
1. Digital commerce (B2B e-commerce platform)
2. Smart manufacturing (IoT, predictive maintenance)
3. Data analytics (customer insights, demand forecasting)
4. Cloud modernization

**Roadmap**:
- **Wave 1**: Launch B2B portal, migrate email to cloud
- **Wave 2**: Implement IoT sensors, build data lake
- **Wave 3**: AI-powered demand forecasting, migrate ERP to cloud

**Stage 3: Technology**

**Cloud Migration**: 
- Rehost: Email and collaboration (Office 365)
- Repurchase: CRM (Salesforce)
- Refactor: Custom applications to Azure
- Retain: ERP (for now, migrate in Year 2)

**API Platform**: Expose product catalog, pricing, order status via APIs

**Stage 4: Process**

**Quote-to-Order Process**:
- **Before**: Customer calls, rep manually creates quote, emails PDF, customer calls back to order
- **After**: Customer logs into portal, configures product, gets instant quote, places orderâ€”all self-service

**Result**: 60% of orders self-service, 40% faster quote-to-order

**Stage 5: Change**

**Training**: 500 sales and service reps trained on new portal and CRM

**Communication**: Monthly town halls, weekly email updates, intranet hub

**Results**: 75% adoption in 3 months, NPS +15 points

## Dialogue Quality Tips

### Creating Natural Flow

Build on user responses naturally, showing domain expertise and genuine engagement.

### Demonstrating Expertise

Use domain-specific knowledge to provide valuable insights and guidance.

### Adaptive Responses

Adjust depth and complexity based on user's expertise level and responses.
