# Examples for Public Relations Strategist

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: B2B SaaS Product Launch PR Campaign

**Stage 1: Objectives**

**Goal**: Generate awareness and leads for new AI product
**Targets**:
- 25 media placements (10 tier-1/2, 15 tier-3/digital)
- 500 inbound leads from media coverage
- 3 customer case studies featured in media

**Target Media**: TechCrunch, VentureBeat, The Information, Forbes, Wall Street Journal, industry blogs

**Stage 2: Story and Messages**

**Core Story**: "AI-powered platform helps enterprises cut customer support costs by 40% while improving satisfaction"

**Key Messages**:
1. "First AI that understands context like a human support agent"
2. "Customers see 40% cost reduction and 30% satisfaction increase"
3. "Up and running in days, not months like competitors"

**Angles**:
- **Tech media**: AI innovation, technical differentiation
- **Business media**: ROI, market opportunity
- **Industry media**: Solving customer support challenge

**Stage 3: Outreach**

**Exclusive**: TechCrunch gets exclusive 48-hour embargo (major tech outlet)

**Pitches**: Personalized pitches to 30 journalists, emphasizing AI trend and customer ROI

**Press release**: Distributed at embargo lift with customer quotes and data

**Stage 4: Thought Leadership**

**CEO byline**: "The Future of Customer Support is AI-Human Collaboration" (Forbes)

**Podcast tour**: CEO on 5 industry podcasts discussing AI trends

**Stage 5: Results**

**Coverage**:
- 32 placements (exceeded goal)
- TechCrunch exclusive + WSJ, Forbes, The Information
- Combined reach: 50M

**Business impact**:
- 712 inbound leads attributed to coverage
- 15% conversion to sales pipeline
- 3 enterprise deals cite media as influencer
