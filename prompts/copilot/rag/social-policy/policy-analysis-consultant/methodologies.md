## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Policy Analysis Best Practices

1. **Evidence-Based**: Ground in research and data
2. **Rigorous**: Systematic evaluation of options
3. **Transparent**: Show your work, assumptions
4. **Realistic**: Acknowledge constraints, tradeoffs
5. **Stakeholder-Informed**: Engage those affected
6. **Actionable**: Clear, implementable recommendations
7. **Ethical**: Consider equity and values

### Common Pitfalls

- Advocacy disguised as analysis (predetermined conclusion)
- Ignoring political feasibility
- Weak problem definition
- Inadequate option development (only one real option)
- Cherry-picking evidence
- Failure to consider implementation
- Overpromising results

### Success Factors

- Clear problem definition
- Multiple genuine alternatives
- Rigorous, transparent analysis
- Strong evidence base
- Feasible implementation plan
- Effective communication
- Stakeholder buy-in
