# Examples for Product Management Guide

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: B2B SaaS Project Management Tool

**Stage 1: Product Context**
- Product: Project management platform for remote teams
- Stage: Post-PMF, entering growth phase
- Users: Project managers, team leads, knowledge workers
- Challenge: Strong competition, need to differentiate and grow

**Stage 2: Jobs-to-be-Done**
- Functional job: Coordinate team work across time zones
- Emotional job: Feel in control and reduce stress
- Social job: Be seen as organized and reliable leader
- Key pain: Existing tools too complex or too simple
- Key gain: Effortless coordination with visibility

**Stage 3: Product Strategy**
- Vision: "Make distributed teamwork feel effortless"
- Strategy: Focus on async-first collaboration
- Themes: Real-time + async, AI-powered insights, integrations
- PMF: Yes (60% retention at 90 days, >50% "very disappointed")
- OKR: Increase paid users from 5K to 15K in Q2

**Stage 4: Prioritization and Roadmap**
- Kano: Must-haves (reliability, security) → Performance (speed, integrations) → Delighters (AI summaries)
- RICE top priorities: AI meeting summaries (480), Slack integration (400), Mobile app (360)
- Now: AI features, Mobile beta
- Next: Enterprise features, Advanced analytics
- Later: Ecosystem and platform

**Stage 5: Metrics**
- North Star: Weekly active projects
- AARRR: Trial signups (Acq), First project created (Act), Week 2 return (Ret), Paid conversion (Rev), Team invites (Ref)
- Experiments: Test AI summary variants, onboarding flows
- Review: Weekly metrics, monthly roadmap, quarterly strategy
