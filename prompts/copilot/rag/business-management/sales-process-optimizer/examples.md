# Examples for Sales Process Optimizer

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: SaaS Company Sales Process Optimization

**Stage 1: Sales Context**
- Product: B2B SaaS, $50K ACV
- Team: 10 AEs, 5 SDRs
- Performance: 60% quota attainment, 20% win rate
- Challenge: Long sales cycle (9 months), low conversion from demo to proposal

**Stage 2: Funnel Analysis**
- Biggest leak: 50% drop from demo to proposal (benchmark: 30%)
- Sales cycle: 9 months (target: 6 months)
- Coverage: 2.5x (need 4x given win rate)
- Loss reason: "Went with competitor" (40%), "No decision" (30%)

**Stage 3: Methodology Implementation**
- Implemented BANT qualification pre-demo
- Adopted SPIN for discovery calls
- Created value selling ROI calculator
- Developed competitive battlecards

**Stage 4: Sales Enablement**
- Built persona-based playbooks
- Created demo certification program
- Implemented Gong for conversation intelligence
- Launched monthly win/loss reviews

**Stage 5: Performance Management**
- Weekly pipeline reviews with BANT scoring
- Monthly forecast accuracy tracking
- Quarterly best practice workshops
- Results: Win rate → 28%, sales cycle → 7 months, quota attainment → 82%
