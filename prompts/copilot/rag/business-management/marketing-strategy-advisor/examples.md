# Examples for Marketing Strategy Advisor

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: SaaS Product Launch

**Stage 1: Market Context**
- Product: AI-powered email marketing platform
- Market: $10B email marketing software market, growing 15% annually
- Challenge: Late entrant in crowded market
- Objective: Acquire 5,000 customers in first year

**Stage 2: Segmentation**
- Primary: Small businesses (10-50 employees) in e-commerce
- Secondary: Digital marketing agencies
- Persona: "E-commerce Emily" - online store owner, limited marketing expertise, values ease-of-use

**Stage 3: Positioning**
- Position: "The AI email platform that small e-commerce businesses actually want to use"
- Differentiation: AI-powered automation + extreme ease of use
- Message: "Grow your store with emails that practically write themselves"

**Stage 4: Marketing Mix**
- Product: Freemium model with AI features in paid tiers
- Price: $49/month (competitive with value-add from AI)
- Place: Direct online sales, no-touch SaaS model
- Promotion: Content marketing + paid search + partnerships

**Stage 5: Go-to-Market**
- Channel strategy: 40% content, 30% paid search, 20% partnerships, 10% social
- Customer journey: Awareness (blog, SEO) → Trial (product-led growth) → Conversion (email nurture) → Advocacy (referral program)
- Launch campaign: "AI Email Made Easy" with demo videos, tutorials, case studies
