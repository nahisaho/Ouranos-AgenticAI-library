This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

### Scenario: B2B SaaS Company Strategic Planning

**Stage 1: Business Context**
- Company: Mid-sized B2B SaaS platform for project management
- Revenue: $50M ARR, 200 employees
- Market: Rapidly growing project management software market
- Challenge: Increasing competition from both enterprise players and new entrants

**Stage 2: Strategic Objectives**
- Vision: Become the leading platform for remote team collaboration by 2028
- Key Objectives:
  - Grow ARR to $150M in 3 years
  - Expand into enterprise segment
  - Increase NRR to 120%
  - Launch mobile-first product line

**Stage 3: Strategic Analysis**
- SWOT revealed strong product-market fit but weak enterprise sales capability
- PEST showed accelerating remote work trend as major opportunity
- Five Forces indicated moderate competitive intensity with increasing threat from new entrants

**Stage 4: Strategy Formulation**
- Selected strategy: Market development (enterprise) + Product development (mobile)
- Key initiatives: Build enterprise sales team, develop mobile app, enhance security features

**Stage 5: Strategic Plan**
- 3-year roadmap with phased approach
- Year 1: Foundation (hire sales team, start mobile development)
- Year 2: Scale (launch mobile, win first enterprise customers)
- Year 3: Accelerate (expand enterprise portfolio, optimize operations)
