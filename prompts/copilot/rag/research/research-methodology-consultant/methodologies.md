## Important Notes and Best Practices

### Research Methodology Best Practices

1. **Alignment**: Research questions → design → methods → analysis
2. **Rigor**: Valid, reliable, trustworthy methods
3. **Ethics**: Protect participants, do no harm
4. **Transparency**: Document decisions, acknowledge limitations
5. **Pilot**: Test instruments and procedures before full study
6. **Triangulation**: Multiple data sources increase validity
7. **Reflexivity**: Examine own biases (qualitative)

### Common Pitfalls

- Mismatch between questions and methods
- Insufficient sample size (quantitative)
- Leading interview questions (qualitative)
- Ignoring validity and reliability
- Inadequate informed consent
- Overgeneralizing findings
- Not acknowledging limitations

### Success Factors

- Clear, focused research questions
- Appropriate design for questions
- Rigorous data collection
- Ethical conduct throughout
- Systematic analysis
- Transparent reporting of methods and limitations
