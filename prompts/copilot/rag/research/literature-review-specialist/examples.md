# Examples for Literature Review Specialist

This file provides concrete usage examples and scenarios demonstrating this prompt in action.

---

### Scenario: Literature Review on Teacher Mentoring and Retention

**Purpose**: Establish foundation for dissertation on mentoring programs

**Stage 1: Scope**
- **Question**: "What is the relationship between mentoring and teacher retention, and what factors moderate this relationship?"
- **Inclusion**: Peer-reviewed, empirical, K-12 teachers, published 2010-2024, English
- **Databases**: ERIC, PsycINFO, Web of Science

**Stage 2: Search**
- **Terms**: ("beginning teacher*" OR "novice teacher*") AND (mentor* OR induction) AND (retention OR turnover)
- **Results**: 1,250 records → 950 after duplicates → 130 full-text assessed → 45 included
- **PRISMA diagram**: Documented process

**Stage 3: Appraisal**
- Quality assessment with MMAT
- 30 high quality, 12 medium, 3 low (excluded)
- Data extraction: Design, sample, findings, effect sizes

**Stage 4: Synthesis**
- **Theme 1**: Impact on retention (meta-analysis: OR = 1.5)
- **Theme 2**: Quality of relationship (qualitative themes: trust, support)
- **Theme 3**: Program features (structured, frequent, subject-match)
- **Gaps**: Few experimental studies, rural schools understudied

**Stage 5: Writing**
- 25-page review, APA format
- Introduction establishes problem
- Three thematic sections with synthesis
- Critical evaluation of methods
- Gaps section justifies dissertation study
- 42 references

**Outcome**: Strong foundation demonstrating knowledge of field and research gap
